Products

Boiled dumplings are a classic Chinese dish made by cooking dumplings in water. The dough is soft, and the filling is juicy. They are often served with dipping sauces like soy sauce, vinegar, or chili oil.

Steamed dumplings are cooked by steaming, which keeps the dumpling skin tender and slightly chewy. This method preserves the flavors of the filling, making them taste fresh and delicate.

Pan-fried dumplings are fried in a pan until the bottoms are crispy and golden, while the tops remain soft. They are loved for their crispy texture and rich flavor.
